Points of Interest In Irondequoit

Food

Explore the best local dining spots, from the nostalgic warmth of Parkside Diner to the lively atmosphere of Shamrock Jack's, and the vibrant culinary experiences at I-Square.

Helmer Nature Center

Discover the peaceful beauty of Helmer Nature Center, a quiet retreat with trails to explore, wildlife to observe, and community events that bring nature and education together.

Durand Eastman Park

Experience the natural beauty of Durand Eastman Park, offering serene hiking trails, fun sledding hills, and the stunning sunsets at Durand Eastman Beach.

Seneca Park Zoo

Get an up-close look at animals from around the world at Seneca Park Zoo, a place where wildlife conservation and education thrive alongside memorable animal encounters.

Seabreeze

Enjoy timeless thrills at Seabreeze Amusement Park, where classic rides, a scenic lake view, and the historic Jack Rabbit roller coaster create unforgettable memories.
